Experience: Worked at a fab shop, producing flamecut metal parts for offshore drilling rigs. I've taught AutoCAD at the local college in Houston, TX. I've designed, installed, documented, and maintained the Ethernet / fibre optic networks in the engineering labs at the NASA Johnson Space Center in what is left of Houston, TX. (The biggest building, (building 10) is where they produce the velcro-laced containers for the ISS as well as the parts for the astronaut jet packs (for EVAs) and parts of the Space Shuttle.) I've also worked on Family Tree websites, with scanning and storing of family photographs and legal documents. Regrets? I've had a few; but then again, too few to mention.

Certifications: B.S. Degree in Electrical Technology, Certified AutoCAD Professional, CompTIA A+ certification.
